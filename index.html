THE STORY📖

A group of Gorillas, tired of everyday monotony, decided to create their own Republic, directly on the Ethereum Chain.⟠
After several debates and more or less cultural exchanges, the right direction stood out before their eyes, with the creation of Gorilla Republic.
the Focus at the base of this Community are to create an ecosystem that allows its participants to obtain a passive income, this will be guaranteed through various prize draws (including 2 Tesla), thanks to the creation of the First NFT Theme Restaurant in Italy , of which part of the profits will be regularly redistributed among the Gorillas.
The Holders will also have the royalities of the mearchandise.

Define the Focus

To ensure each minter the security of having an effective return from the project, we have studied every single step in the short and long term to maintain an excellent level of both rewards and value of the NFT floor.
